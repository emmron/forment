Formet: Email as Endpoint

How it works: Just use your email as the form endpoint. No signup required. Submissions are sent directly to that email.

Try it out

The Code

It's just a regular HTML form. No JavaScript required.

<form action="https://formet.io/f/your@email.com" method="POST">
  <input type="text" name="name" required>
  <input type="email" name="email" required>
  <textarea name="message"></textarea>
  <button type="submit">Send</button>
</form>
